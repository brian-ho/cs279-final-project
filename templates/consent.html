Statement of Informed Consent

You're about to participate in a research study. Your contribution to our research allows us to learn how to crowdsource writing effective wayfinding descriptions.

Please read the following information carefully before proceeding.

Why we are doing this research

We are trying to understand how to create a crowdsourcing system that results in effective wayfinding descriptions, and what the attributes of effective descriptions are.

What you will have to do

You will be shown one of three tasks that ask you to find an object based on a description, edit the description, or assess or rank the work of others.

Potential risks

There are no risks anticipated in taking part in this study and you are free to leave at any time.

Duration

Approximately 10 minutes.
